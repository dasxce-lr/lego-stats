LEGO 10297 Boutique Hotel is a 3,066 piece Icons exclusive set with 7 minifigs released in 2022. It is a luxurious hotel inspired by turn-of-the-century European architecture built out of 5 sections which consist of guest rooms and penthouse suite, lobby, terrace and staircase. It celebrates fifteen years of the Modular Buildings line.

The current retail price is $229.99, however, the average sales listings on the secondary markets is closer to $241. The set is estimated to retire sometime within mid to late 2025. Once retired, the expected annual growth will be close to 9% after the second year, which will value the set between $292 and $317 shortly after it is retired.

Set Facts

  • This celebrates fifteen years of the Modular Buildings line.
  • The set's 7 minifigures includes a bellhop, receptionist, coffee vendor and gallery owner among the collection of hotel guests and staff. The hotel staff have unique uniforms.
  • The Boutique Hotel comes in a large tab-sealed box with standard Adult Collector theming. The upper left corner has a 15 years celebratory badge, along with a five-star rating for the hotel.
  • The price of the set was increased from $199.99 to $229.99 RRP in August 2022.

About Icons Modular Buildings

The LEGO ICONS Modular Buildings subtheme is an extension of the Advanced Models theme. Modular Buildings features large, detailed, connectable buildings, aimed at adults and teenagers.

Modular Buildings currently consists of 10 sets that as of now has run from 2013 to 2022. As of today, 10251 Brick Bank is the most valuable Modular Buildings set with an estimated new/factory sealed value of $569.
Read more
Imagine spending quality time at a luxurious hotel. Where every detail is carefully considered. Where every guests request is met in style. Now you can build your own hotel getaway with this LEGO® Boutique Hotel (10297) model building project for adults.

Enjoy hours of immersive building as you craft the models 5 sections. With unusual triangular geometry and elegant décor, its inspired by opulent turn-of-the-century European architecture. Build the guest rooms and penthouse suite, lobby, terrace and staircase. Then explore all the stories packed within, including fun tributes to other models in the LEGO Modular Buildings collection. With 5 sections, including the roof with its decorative dome and skylight, plus never-seen-before elements and colors, its sure to delight adult builders.
